About Wild Little Pets

Dedicated to solving exotic pet problems with love and expertise

Our Story

Wild Little Pets was born from a simple belief: every exotic pet deserves expert care. As long-time keepers of lizards, geckos, hedgehogs, and other wild little companions, we saw too many owners struggling with the same problems – from incorrect lighting to dietary issues, and even life-threatening impactions.

That's why we created this platform – to share real, proven solutions that turn pet struggles into success stories.

What We Do

We write detailed, problem-solving blogs that cover:

  • 🦎 Bearded dragon, leopard gecko & crested gecko care
  • 🦔 Hedgehog hibernation & quill loss solutions
  • 🐍 Reptile lighting & heating mistakes to avoid
  • 🐸 Bioactive setups & emergency first aid

Every guide is based on research, real experience, and input from exotic veterinarians.

Our Commitment

We will never recommend a product we don't trust. Our affiliate links help keep this site running, but we only partner with brands that genuinely improve the lives of wild little pets.

Thank you for being part of our journey. Together, we can give our scaly, spiky, and slippery friends the care they deserve.

– The Wild Little Pets Team 🦎
